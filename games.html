Games

All of the current games made by us are Game Jam prototypes.

We have yet to attempt a full fledged game.

Epic Dungeon Quest

Epic Dungeon Quest is our studios first game as a team in the same room. It took a little under 45 hours to make for the Epic Game Jam. Its a platformer about getting to the end of each level without dying.

Snow

Snow is a visual novel made for the Public Domain Jam solo by Pace Marier. The game was made from scratch in 4 days and is CC0. Its based off of the Brothers Grimm fairytale Snow-white with a couple of twists.

Elevatr

Are you ready for the craziest elevator ride of your life? Elevatr is a game made for the Ludum Dare 30 Jam by Andrew Schwan and Pace Marier. The game took a little under 70 hours to make.
